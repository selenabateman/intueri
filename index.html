About

Selena Bateman is a Registered Social Worker (MSW) with over 20 years in social services across clinical, regulatory, and adjudicative settings.

Intueri Wellness offers limited pro bono counselling offering focused on supporting adult women navigating recovery from domestic violence and post-abuse stabilization.

This work is grounded in trauma-informed principles and evidence-based approaches, with an emphasis on stabilization, emotional regulation, and rebuilding self-trust.

This counselling work is offered independently of any government role and is not connected to employment.

Scope

  • Pro bono trauma-informed counselling
  • Support related to recovery from domestic violence
  • Psychoeducation on trauma, coercive control, and emotional regulation
  • Post-abuse stabilization and grounding
  • Identity rebuilding and life transitions
