Woman-centred. Trauma-informed. Evidence-based.

Intueri Wellness provides pro bono, individual support for women 25 years of age and older who self-identify as having experienced intimate partner violence, coercive control, psychological abuse, or post-separation harm.

Therapeutic Support & Scope of Support

At Intueri Wellness, counseling is not about re-living what happened.

We focus on understanding the impact of abuse.
We prioritize safety and stability.
We develop the skills needed to move forward with clarity and confidence.

Support is trauma-informed and stabilization-oriented following intimate partner violence, coercive control, and post-separation harm.

Our work is structured, practical, and evidence-based.

Therapeutic interventions draw on the When Love Hurts curriculum, Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT), Dialectical Behaviour Therapy (DBT), and psychosomatic interventions.

Our work together includes:

  • Understanding patterns of coercive control and abuse dynamics
  • Identifying and reducing trauma-driven emotional and cognitive responses
  • Reducing shame, self-blame, and internalized responsibility
  • Building emotional regulation and distress-tolerance skills
  • Supporting self-trust, agency, and forward momentum

The work emphasizes restoring agency, supporting decision-making capacity, and reducing the ongoing impacts of coercive control over time.

Important Information

Intueri Wellness is not an emergency or crisis service and does not provide crisis intervention, emergency response, or medical care. If you are in immediate danger or require urgent support, contact emergency services in your area.

This website provides general information about scope and eligibility.

This support is offered independently and is not connected to any government role or employment.
