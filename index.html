Intueri Wellness

Intueri Wellness is a pro bono, trauma-informed counselling offering focused on supporting adult women affected by domestic violence, including experiences of coercive control, psychological abuse, and post-separation harm.

Scope of Support

The scope of this counselling offering is informed by trauma-focused frameworks addressing domestic violence and coercive control. Interventions may draw on the When Love Hurts curriculum, with attention to power dynamics, patterns of psychological abuse, and the impact of coercive control over time.

The work may include psychoeducation related to trauma responses associated with domestic violence, including the cognitive, emotional, and physiological effects of prolonged interpersonal harm. Stabilization-oriented interventions are used consistent with advanced trauma-informed practice, including principles aligned with Trauma Professional II–level training.

Skills-based interventions informed by cognitive behavioural therapy (CBT) and dialectical behaviour therapy (DBT) may be incorporated, with an emphasis on emotional regulation, distress tolerance, grounding, and behavioural stabilization. Mind–body and psychosomatic approaches may also be used to support nervous system regulation and post-trauma recovery.

Support may also address significant life transitions related to safety, separation, and recovery following experiences of interpersonal violence.

This offering is non-crisis and outpatient in nature.

Important Information

Intueri Wellness is not an emergency or crisis service and does not provide crisis intervention, emergency response, or medical care. Individuals experiencing immediate risk or danger should contact emergency services or a local crisis line.

This counselling work is offered independently and is not connected to any government role or employment.
