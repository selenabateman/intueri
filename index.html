Intueri Wellness provides limited, pro bono, individual support for women 25 years of age and older who self-identify as having experienced intimate partner violence, coercive control, psychological abuse, or post-separation harm.

Woman-centred. Trauma-informed. Evidence-based.

Scope of Support

At Intueri Wellness, counseling is not about endlessly re-living what happened. We focus on understanding the impact of abuse. We prioritize safety and stability. We develop the skills needed to move forward with clarity and confidence.

Support is trauma-informed and stabilization-oriented following intimate partner violence, coercive control, and post-separation harm.

Our work is structured, practical, and evidence-based.

Interventions draw on the When Love Hurts curriculum, Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT), Dialectical Behaviour Therapy (DBT), and nervous-system regulation and psychosomatic interventions.

  • understanding patterns of coercive control and abuse dynamics
  • reducing shame, self-blame, and internalized responsibility
  • building emotional regulation and distress-tolerance skills during periods of instability

The work focuses on restoring agency, supporting decision-making capacity, and reducing the functional impacts of coercive control over time.

Important Information

Intueri Wellness is not an emergency or crisis service and does not provide crisis intervention, emergency response, or medical care. If you are in immediate danger or require urgent support, contact emergency services in your area.

This website provides general information about scope and eligibility.

This support is offered independently and is not connected to any government role or employment.
