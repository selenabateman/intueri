Intueri Wellness

Trauma-informed, evidence-informed counselling support for adult women navigating recovery from domestic violence, identity rebuilding, and major life transitions.

Selena Bateman, MSW, RSW · Registered Social Worker (Ontario & British Columbia)

About

I’m Selena Bateman, a Registered Social Worker (MSW) with over 20 years of experience in social services across clinical, regulatory, and adjudicative settings.

Intueri Wellness is a pro bono counselling offering focused on supporting adult women navigating recovery from domestic violence and post-abuse stabilization.

This work is grounded in trauma-informed principles and evidence-based approaches, with an emphasis on emotional stabilization, rebuilding self-trust, and supporting clarity during periods of significant transition.

This counselling work is offered independently of my government role and is not connected to my employment.

Scope of Support

  • Pro bono trauma-informed counselling
  • Support related to recovery from domestic violence
  • Psychoeducation regarding trauma, coercive control, and emotional regulation
  • Post-abuse stabilization and grounding
  • Identity rebuilding and major life transitions
Contact information is provided for professional identification purposes only: selena@intueri.ca
This is a non-crisis, outpatient counselling offering. It does not replace emergency or crisis services. If you are in immediate danger, contact emergency services or a local crisis line.
