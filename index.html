Intueri Wellness

Trauma-informed counselling and wellness support for women navigating recovery, identity rebuilding, and major life transitions.

About

Intueri Wellness offers thoughtful, evidence-informed support grounded in trauma-informed practice. Services are designed for adult women seeking stabilization, clarity, and long-term resilience, particularly following intimate partner violence or significant life disruption.

Intueri Wellness provides non-crisis, outpatient support. Services are independent of any government role and are not a replacement for emergency or crisis services.

Services

  • Pro bono trauma-informed counselling (limited availability)
  • Domestic violence recovery support and psychoeducation
  • Emotional regulation and post-abuse stabilization
  • Support through life transitions and identity rebuilding
