Intueri Wellness offers pro bono counseling offering focused on supporting adult women affected by domestic violence, coercive control, psychological abuse, and post-separation harm.

Scope of Support

Counseling is informed by trauma-focused addressing the impacts of domestic violence and coercive control. The work is stabilization-oriented and focused on supporting safety, clarity, and psychological recovery following interpersonal harm.

Interventions draw on the When Love Hurts curriculum, Cognitive Behavioural Therapy, and Dialectical Behavioural Therapy to support:

  • emotion regulation following chronic stress and coercive control and restructuring of cognitive patterns
  • distress tolerance during periods of instability or post-separation harm
  • interpersonal effectiveness in the context of power imbalance and boundary erosion
  • grounding and present-focused strategies to reduce trauma-related dysregulation

The focus is on restoring agency, supporting decision-making capacity, and reducing the impacts of coercive control over time.

Important Information

Intueri Wellness is not an emergency or crisis service and does not provide crisis intervention, emergency response, or medical care. Individuals experiencing immediate risk or danger should contact emergency services or a local crisis line.

This counselling work is offered independently and is not connected to any government role or employment.
