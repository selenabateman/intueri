Woman-centred. Trauma-informed. Evidence-based.

Intueri Wellness provides limited, pro bono, individual support for women 25 years of age and older who self-identify as having experienced intimate partner violence, coercive control, psychological abuse, or post-separation harm.

Scope of Support

Support is trauma-informed and stabilization-oriented, with attention to safety, structure, clarity, and post-abuse stabilization following intimate partner violence and coercive control.

Interventions draw on the When Love Hurts curriculum, CBT-informed approaches, and DBT skills training to support:

  • emotion regulation and cognitive restructuring
  • distress tolerance during periods of instability, including post-separation harm
  • interpersonal effectiveness where power imbalance and boundary erosion are present
  • grounding and present-focused strategies to reduce trauma-related dysregulation

The work focuses on strengthening agency, supporting decision-making capacity, and reducing the functional impacts of coercive control over time.

Important Information

Intueri Wellness is not an emergency or crisis service and does not provide crisis intervention, emergency response, or medical care. If you are in immediate danger or require urgent support, contact emergency services in your area.

This website provides general information about scope and eligibility.

This support is offered independently and is not connected to any government role or employment.
