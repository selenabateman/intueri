Intueri Wellness is pro bono counseling focused on supporting adult women affected by domestic violence, including experiences of coercive control, psychological abuse, and post-separation harm.

Scope of Support

Counselling is informed by trauma-focused frameworks addressing domestic violence and coercive control. Interventions draw on the When Love Hurts curriculum, with attention to power dynamics, patterns of psychological abuse, and the impact of coercive control over time.

Interventions also include psychoeducation related to trauma responses associated with domestic violence, including the cognitive, emotional, and physiological effects of prolonged interpersonal harm.

Important Information

Intueri Wellness is not an emergency or crisis service and does not provide crisis intervention, emergency response, or medical care. Individuals experiencing immediate risk or danger should contact emergency services or a local crisis line.

This counselling work is offered independently and is not connected to any government role or employment.
